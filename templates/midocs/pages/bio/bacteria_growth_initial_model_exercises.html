Exercise 1

Following the procedure outlined in the {% intlink bacteria_growth_initial_model %}bacteria model page{%endintlink%}, compute $B_1$, $B_2$, and $B_3$ for the following discrete dynamical systems. \begin{align*} {\rm{a.}} \hspace{2mm} B_0 & = 4 & \hspace{5mm} B_{t+1} - B_t & = 0.5 \times B_t \\ {\rm{b.}} \hspace{2mm} B_0 & = 4 & \hspace{5mm} B_{t+1} - B_t & = 0.1 \times B_t \\ {\rm{c.}} \hspace{2mm} B_0 & = 0.2 & \hspace{5mm} B_{t+1} - B_t & = 0.05 \times B_t \\ {\rm{d.}} \hspace{2mm} B_0 & = 0.2 & \hspace{5mm} B_{t+1} - B_t & = 1 \times B_t \\ {\rm{e.}} \hspace{2mm} B_0 & = 100 & \hspace{5mm} B_{t+1} - B_t & = 0.4 \times B_t \\ {\rm{f.}} \hspace{2mm} B_0 & = 100 & \hspace{5mm} B_{t+1} - B_t & = 0.01 \times B_t \end{align*}

Exercise 3

When {% intlink bacteria_growth_initial_model linear_model%}developing the linear model{%endintlink%}, observe that we plotted $B_{t+1} - B_t$ versus $B_t$. The points are $(B_0, B_1-B_0)$, $(B_1, B_2-B_1)$, etc. The second coordinate, $B_{t+1} - B_t$ is the population increase during time period $t$, given that the population at the beginning of the time period is $B_t$. Explain why the point (0,0) would be a point of this graph.
