The phase space of a {% intlink "dynamical_system_definition" %}dynamical system{%endintlink%} is another word for the {% intlink "state_space_definition" %}state space{%endintlink%}, which is the set of all possible states of the system. The term phase space is often used when the state space is continuous and finite-dimensional.
