A dependent variable is a {% intlink variable_definition%}variable{%endintlink%} that represents a quantity that changes based on other quantities being manipulated in an experiment. A {% intlink independent_variable_definition%}independent variable{%endintlink%} represents a quantity that is being manipulated directly.

In the context of a {% intlink function_definition%}function{%endintlink%}, the independent variables are the inputs to the function and the dependent variables are the outputs of the function.
