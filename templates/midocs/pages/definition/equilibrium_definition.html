An equilibrium of a {% intlink "dynamical_system_definition" %}dynamical system{%endintlink%} is a value of the {% intlink "state_variable_definition" %}state variables{% endintlink%} where the state variables do not change. This means if the systems starts at an equilibrium, the state will remain at the equilibrium forever.
