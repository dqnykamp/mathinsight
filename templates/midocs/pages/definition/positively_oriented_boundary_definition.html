A boundary of a surface is positively {% intlink "parametrized_surface_orient" %}oriented{%endintlink%} if its direction corresponds to the fingers of your right hand when your thumb points in the direction of the surface {% intlink "parametrized_surface_normal_vector" %}normal{%endintlink%}. Equivalently, if you walk near the edge on the {% intlink "positive_side_definition" %}positive side{%endintlink%} of the surface in the direction corresponding to the orientation of boundary, then surface must be to your left and the edge must be to your right.
