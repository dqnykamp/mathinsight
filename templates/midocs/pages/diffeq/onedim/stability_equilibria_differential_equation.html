Stability theorem

Let $\diff{x}{t} = f(x)$ be an autonomous differential equation. Suppose $x(t)=x^*$ is an equilibrium, i.e., $f(x^*)=0$. Then

  • if $f'(x*)<0$, the equilibrium $x(t)=x^*$ is stable, and
  • if $f'(x^*)>0$, the equilibrium $x(t)=x^*$ is unstable.
